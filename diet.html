  • Eat Less: Avoid overeating. Hunger builds discipline.
  • Simple Foods: Whole grains, vegetables, nuts, and clean proteins.
  • Water Over Wine: Stay hydrated; avoid excessive indulgence.
  • Fasting: Skip meals to strengthen willpower and focus.
  • No Luxury: Avoid processed, sugary, or excessive comfort foods.
  • Eat with Gratitude: Appreciate each meal as fuel, not entertainment.
